### 2пёҸвғЈ RTK вҶ’ Ras вҶ’ MAPK (Q024)
```
Growth factor вҶ’ RTK (phosphorylated)
             вҶ“ recruits GRB2-SOS adapter
             вҶ’ SOS catalyzes GDPвҶ’GTP on Ras
             вҶ’ Ras-GTP recruits Raf (MAPKKK)
             вҶ’ Raf phosphorylates MEK (MAPKK)
             вҶ’ MEK phosphorylates ERK (MAPK)
             вҶ’ Nuclear вҶ’ Gene expression

        GAP CAN'T RECOGNIZE G12V вҶ’ STUCK ON вҶ’ uncontrolled division
```
**Key**: Cascade = amplification. Ras is master regulator.

### 3пёҸвғЈ Wnt/Notch вҶ’ Proteolysis (Q032, Q013)
```
NOTCH PATHWAY:
Delta ligand вҶ’ Notch
            вҶ“ S1 cleavage (TNFОұ convertase)
            вҶ’ S2 cleavage (ADAM protease - MEMBRANE)
            вҶ’ S3 cleavage (Оі-secretase - INSIDE)
            вҶ’ NICD freed вҶ’ nucleus with RBPj вҶ’ transcription

Mnemonic: "TAN" = TNF-convertase, ADAM, Оі-secretase

WNT/ОІ-CATENIN:
Wnt вҶ’ Frizzled+LRP
    вҶ“ Disrupts APC-Axin complex
    вҶ’ ОІ-catenin NOT phosphorylated
    вҶ’ ОІ-catenin accumulates (NOT degraded)
    вҶ’ ОІ-catenin вҶ’ nucleus with TCF/LEF
    вҶ’ "GO DIVIDE" genes on

CANCER: APC lost вҶ’ Can't form destruction complex вҶ’ ОІ-catenin always high
```
**Key**: Notch = proteolysis (3 cuts). Wnt = prevent degradation (save protein).

### 4пёҸвғЈ TGF-ОІ вҶ’ SMAD (Q040)
```
TGF-ОІ вҶ’ TОІRII (constitutive) вҶ’ TОІRI (kinase activated)
      вҶ’ Phosphorylates SMAD2/3 (R-SMADs)
      вҶ’ pSMAD2/3 + SMAD4 (Co-SMAD) = trimer
      вҶ’ Nuclear with TCF/LEF
      вҶ’ p15/p21 (CDK inhibitors) вҶ‘ вҶ’ CELL CYCLE STOP

DUAL ROLE:
- Early cancer (wild-type): STOPS division (tumor suppressor) вң“
- Late cancer (SMAD4 lost): Promotes EMT + metastasis (tumor promoter) вң—

KEY DIFFERENCE FROM BMP:
BMP-2 uses SMAD1/5/8 (different R-SMADs) + SMAD4
вҶ’ Same SMAD4, different R-SMADs = selectivity!
```
**Key**: TGF-ОІ can suppress OR promote cancer (context = mutations present).

## рҹ§  MEMORY TRICKS

### Distinguish Similar Proteins:
```
SMAD2 vs SMAD3:     Both activated by TGF-ОІ, but SMAD3вҶ’activation, SMAD2вҶ’repression
SMAD2/3 vs SMAD4:   SMAD4 is Co-SMAD (works with BOTH R-SMADs)
SMAD vs IDRP:       I-SMAD (6,7) = Inhibitors! SMAD7 blocks TGF-ОІ
```

### Distinguish Similar PATHWAYS:
```
GPCR (Gs):     Hormone вҶ’ 2nd messenger (cAMP) вҶ’ PKA phosphorylates
RTK:           Growth factor вҶ’ adapter вҶ’ kinase cascade вҶ’ nuclear TF
TGF-ОІ:         Cytokine вҶ’ SMAD recruitment вҶ’ SMAD complex вҶ’ nuclear
Wnt/Notch:     Proteolysis (cut protein) вҶ’ release intracellular domain вҶ’ nuclear
```

### Three ways to STOP a pathway:
```
1. PHOSPHATASE: Removes phosphate (PP1, MKP, PTP)
2. GAP/GTPase: Converts GTPвҶ’GDP (stops Ras, G-proteins)
3. UBIQUITIN: Marks for destruction (IОәBОұ, ОІ-catenin, NICD)
```

## рҹ§Ә "TEACH-BACK" SELF-CHECK (2 min per topic)

After you STUDY, try to TEACH without notes:

**Q: Explain why Ras-G12V is LOCKED in GTP state**
Your answer should include:
- G12V is at amino acid position 12
- Prevents GAP recognition
- GAP normally removes GTP phosphate
- With G12V, GAP can't remove phosphate вҶ’ GTP stays
- Result: Ras always signals (no OFF switch)

**Q: Why does SMAD7 make TGF-ОІ pro-cancer?**
Your answer should include:
- SMAD7 is INHIBITOR (not activator like SMAD2/3)
- Blocks TОІRI from phosphorylating SMAD2/3
- Breaking canonical pathway вҶ’ non-canonical EMT pathway activates
- EMT = epithelial to mesenchymal = cell migration = metastasis

If you can explain these, you understand the MECHANISM.
If you just memorize "G12V = cancer", exam will fail you.

## рҹҡЁ TOP EXAM TRAPS

| Trap | Why Students Fall | How to Avoid |
|------|------------------|--------------|
| "GTP = active" always assumed true | Most proteins, but not ALL | Ask: Does this protein have GTPase activity? Or is GTP catalyptically stuck? |
| Confuse "pathway activated" with "cell divides" | Students think activation = instant division | Remember: MAPK activated, but needs sustaining signals, takes TIME |
| Think Wnt/Notch are similar | Both use "intracellular domains" | NO! Wnt saves ОІ-catenin (prevent degradation), Notch produces NICD (proteolysis) |
| Assume tumor = more growth signals | Actually often = loss of brakes + gain of gas pedal | Example: APC loss = BOTH tumor suppressor loss + Wnt pathway on |
| Forgot what STOPS signaling | Students explain only the "on" part | EXAM MARK: Writing what turns signal OFF gets +20% bonus |

## рҹ“ұ LAST-SECOND MENTAL TRICKS

- **GPCR = 7 transmembrane** (draw 7 wiggles = 7 helices crossing membrane)
- **cAMP = second messenger** (molecule, NOT protein like GОұs)
- **Ras = adapter GTPase**, NOT G-protein (different families!)
- **SMAD = mother of transcription** (MOM = "mediates outside/inside" = SMAD)
- **ОІ-catenin = accumulates** (Wnt stops destruction, NOT makes new)
- **NICD = nucleus-acting domain** (answer is in the NAME: intracellular domain)
- **APC = "almost protein-counting"** (lost = loses ability to count/phosphorylate ОІ-catenin)
- **NF-ОәB = "nuclear factor Оә-light chain enhancer of B cells"** (originally found in B cells)
